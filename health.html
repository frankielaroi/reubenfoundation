Healthcare Program

Promoting Access, Awareness, and Innovation for Healthier Communities

At Reuben Foundation Africa, we believe that good health is the foundation for a thriving society. Our Healthcare Program focuses on improving access to quality, affordable healthcare for underserved communities across the continent especially youth, women, and vulnerable populations.

We deliver health-focused interventions that are community-driven, preventive, and supported by innovation. From clinics to classrooms, we are working to close the health gap through outreach, education, and scalable solutions.

Our Key Focus Areas

Primary Healthcare Access

We support medical outreach initiatives, mobile clinics, and health screenings that deliver essential care to rural and low-income communities that lack basic health infrastructure.

Maternal and Child Health

We promote safe motherhood and early childhood wellness by supporting prenatal care, trained birth attendants, nutrition education, and community-based maternal health programs.

Preventive Health Education

We run awareness campaigns and school-based programs on hygiene, nutrition, sexual health, and the prevention of common diseases such as malaria, HIV, and hepatitis.

Mental Health Awareness and Support

We advocate for mental health inclusion through counseling support, youth-friendly wellness spaces, digital resources, and community conversations aimed at ending stigma.

Telehealth and HealthTech Innovation

We explore and support technology-based solutions that improve health delivery, including mobile diagnostics, teleconsultations, and digital health literacy.

Clean Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH)

We collaborate with communities to improve access to clean water, build sanitation facilities, and promote hygiene practices in schools and homes.

Health Financing Support

We offer limited assistance to students, families, or individuals facing urgent health challenges, helping cover basic treatment or surgery costs through a needs-based support system.

International Nurse Job Placement Program

We connect qualified African nurses to global healthcare systems, particularly in the U.S., U.K., and other high-demand regions, through strategic partnerships with international recruiters and hospitals.

This initiative includes support with:

  • Application and documentation
  • NCLEX and IELTS training guidance
  • Visa sponsorship and relocation support
  • Cultural orientation and career mentoring

We aim to empower African nurses with global opportunities while helping to bridge global healthcare workforce gaps.

Our healthcare initiatives are built on partnerships with local clinics, health professionals, tech innovators, international recruiters, and grassroots organizations to ensure culturally relevant, lasting impact.

Together, we are building healthier, stronger communities across Africa and creating global pathways for African healthcare talent.
