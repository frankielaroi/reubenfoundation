Entrepreneurship Program

Africa's future depends on bold ideas brought to life by empowered youth. We help young African innovators turn their ideas into sustainable, investment-ready businesses.

Through training, mentorship, and support in areas like business planning, legal registration, and market access, we focus on building ventures that drive economic growth and job creation.

Our Entrepreneurship Mission

The Entrepreneurship Program by Reuben Foundation Africa is designed to help young innovators, creatives, and aspiring entrepreneurs transform their ideas into sustainable ventures that can attract investment and drive meaningful impact.

Our Approach

This program is not about simply handing out funds. Instead, it provides the tools, mentorship, and strategic support young Africans need to build bankable, investor-ready businesses that solve problems, create jobs, and drive inclusive growth.

We focus on building ventures in agriculture, technology, and creative industries that have the potential to scale and create lasting impact across African communities.

What We Offer

Comprehensive support to transform your ideas into investment-ready businesses

Business Development Training

Participants are trained to craft strong business plans, build scalable models, and create compelling pitch decks tailored for real-world funding opportunities.

Legal & Formalization Support

We provide support with business registration, intellectual property (IP) protection, and access to legal experts to ensure that ventures are well-structured and protected.

Investment Readiness & Market Access

Selected entrepreneurs are connected to global investors, development funds, and accelerators. We also guide ventures on how to scale operations and enter local, regional, or global markets.

Mentorship & Coaching

Each entrepreneur is paired with experienced mentors who provide one-on-one coaching in business strategy, branding, leadership, and operational planning.

Creative Grants for Musicians & Artists

We offer tailored support to upcoming musicians, artists, and cultural entrepreneurs. This includes small grants, branding and production support, business coaching, and guidance on turning creative talent into sustainable careers.

Pitch Platforms & Exposure

Outstanding ventures are invited to pitch events, innovation showcases, and media features for visibility and potential funding opportunities.
