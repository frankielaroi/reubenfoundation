Scholarship & Mentorship for African Students

Reuben Foundation Africa is preparing to launch its Scholarships & Mentorships Program, an initiative designed to support talented but underserved students across Africa, particularly those from rural and low-income communities. This upcoming program reflects our commitment to breaking the barriers that prevent many bright young minds from accessing quality education and professional guidance.

Through this initiative, selected students will receive comprehensive financial support that covers all key aspects of their education. This includes tuition fees (the cost of instruction and academic teaching), school fees (covering services such as exams, facility use, and extracurricular activities), learning materials (such as textbooks, stationery, and digital tools), and where necessary, accommodation or hostel fees to ensure a safe and stable living environment near their school or institution.

Beyond financial assistance, the program will offer mentorship opportunities with experienced professionals in diverse fields such as technology, business, healthcare, engineering, and agriculture. We recognize agriculture as a powerful tool for economic transformation and sustainability, and we aim to inspire a new generation of youth to see farming and agribusiness as viable and impactful careers.

In addition to mentorship, the program will also provide academic guidance and career counseling to help students make informed decisions about their education and future paths. Our goal is to create a holistic support system that not only helps students stay in school but also equips them with the tools and mindset needed to thrive in the modern world.

Goal

Empower the next generation of African leaders by removing financial and social barriers to education.

While the program is not yet live, preparations are underway for a pilot launch.
