    Football Talent Sourcing & International Placement

    We help discover and connect rising football talent from Africa to top football academies and professional teams in the United States, Europe, and Asia.

    Through our extensive network of scouts, coaches, and local partners, we identify young footballers who show exceptional skill, discipline, and potential. Our goal is to give these players access to world-class training environments and career opportunities that can transform their lives both on and off the field.

    What We Offer

    • Talent Scouting Across Africa: We actively scout in schools, football clubs, local tournaments, and communities across the continent. Weâ€™re focused on identifying boys and girls between the ages of 12 and 21 who demonstrate outstanding talent and commitment.
    • Connection to Academies and Clubs Abroad: Once selected, we work to connect players with trusted football academies and teams in countries like the United States, United Kingdom, Spain, Germany, Italy, Portugal, Turkey, Qatar, and more.     • End-to-End Support: We assist with everything from video profiling and documentation to travel planning, visa guidance, and player orientation. If you are passionate about football and want to help young players succeed, you can register to become an official scout with us.
