Entrepreneurship Program

Africa's future depends on bold ideas brought to life by empowered youth

Africa's future depends on bold ideas brought to life by empowered youth. The Entrepreneurship Program by Reuben Foundation Africa is designed to help young innovators, creatives, and aspiring entrepreneurs transform their ideas into sustainable ventures that can attract investment and drive meaningful impact.

This program is not about simply handing out funds. Instead, it provides the tools, mentorship, and strategic support young Africans need to build bankable, investor-ready businesses that solve problems, create jobs, and drive inclusive growth.

What We Offer

Business Development Training

Participants are trained to craft strong business plans, build scalable models, and create compelling pitch decks tailored for real-world funding opportunities.

Legal and Formalization Support

We provide support with business registration, intellectual property (IP) protection, and access to legal experts to ensure that ventures are well-structured and protected.

Investment Readiness and Market Access

Selected entrepreneurs are connected to global investors, development funds, and accelerators. We also guide ventures on how to scale operations and enter local, regional, or global markets.

Mentorship and Coaching

Each entrepreneur is paired with experienced mentors who provide one-on-one coaching in business strategy, branding, leadership, and operational planning.

Creative Grants for Musicians and Artists

We offer tailored support to upcoming musicians, artists, and cultural entrepreneurs. This includes small grants, branding and production support, business coaching, and guidance on how to turn creative talent into sustainable careers or ventures.

Pitch Platforms and Exposure

Outstanding ventures are invited to pitch events, innovation showcases, and media features for visibility and potential funding.

Who Can Apply

This program is ideal for:

Participants of our Research Fund with ideas ready for commercialization

Youth with early-stage businesses or product prototypes

Creatives, musicians, artists, and tech founders seeking to grow and formalize their ventures

Social entrepreneurs and innovators with solutions but limited access to business experience or investor networks

How to Apply

To express your interest in the Entrepreneurship Program, please fill out the Contact Us form on our website. A member of our team will reach out with the next steps.

At Reuben Foundation Africa, our entrepreneurship and innovation programs are not just about launching businesses — they are about empowering people, transforming communities, and catalyzing sustainable development across the continent. We recognize that Africa’s greatest resource is its people, especially the vibrant, creative, and determined youth who are ready to lead change.

Through a unique blend of technology, capacity building, access to funding, and mentorship, we are nurturing a new generation of problem-solvers, job creators, and visionary leaders. Our initiatives are designed to meet entrepreneurs where they are — whether in remote villages or bustling cities — and equip them with the tools, networks, and mindset to build ventures that solve real challenges in health, education, agriculture, finance, and beyond.

We focus on inclusive innovation, ensuring that men/women, persons with disabilities, and underserved communities have equal access to opportunities that can uplift not only individuals but entire economies. From bootcamps and startup incubation to pitch competitions and policy advocacy, we’re creating a thriving ecosystem where bold ideas are supported and scaled.

By investing in African-led innovation, we are investing in Africa’s future — a future defined by resilience, equity, and homegrown solutions that can shape the world.
